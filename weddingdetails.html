Date:  Friday, 9th of August 2024

Time:  9am till Midnight

Location:  Boturich Castle ("Bow Turr Ock")

2pm-6pm: Complimentary canapes and champagne will be available throughout the day, with speeches to take place before the 3 course dinner with drinks included.
